Morning at the Insectarium

It’s opening shift at the museum. You enter the prep room, flip on the soft lights, and check the day’s schedule.

The habitats need attention before visitors arrive. Since you are in first, you can choose which group of insects to work with. Which group do you choose?

I would like to work with the beetles and worms.

I would like to work with the para-phasmids.

I would like to work with the leafcutter ant colony.

I would like to work with the butterflies in the vivarium.
